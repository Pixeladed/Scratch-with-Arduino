How To:

Build an Arduino Robot:

Now that we’ve learnt how to program and Arduino to blink an LED light (at different rates), produce sound using the Piezo buzzer, and control servo motors, we can now combine all these skills to make our very own Arduino robot! You will need the following equipment to contruct a robot of you own:

  1. Arduino motherboard
  2. Parallax board of education shield
  3. 1 LED light
  4. 1 Piezo buzzer
  5. 2 Servo motors
  6. 5 batteries
  7. 2 Wheels

In order to make this robot function, students must follow a 2-part process to program and construct the robot.

Building the Robot

The robot you will be given will look something like this:

From here, in order to plug in the motors, do what is shown in the picture. Plug both cables in Servo Motor digital port 13 and 12, the left wheel’s cable goes into 13 and right goes into 12, look at the robot from the back when plugging in. Make sure that the black wire is the one closest to the white board (breadboard).

If you have an LED with out a wire, looks like this:

Plug the long end in digital port 4 on the white board (breadboard) and the short end in the ground port (labelled GND).

If you have the one with wires use the same LED light from before and plug the yellow wire into the ground port (labelled GND) and the red wire in port 4.

For the Piezo buzzer simply plug the 3 prongs in digital ports 9, 10 and 11 on the breadboard. Make sure the buzzer is facing forwards.
